Who Am I?

B.Tech in Computer Science (AI & ML) graduate and first-year MBA (BM) student at Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar. Passionate about leveraging technology, strategy, and analytical thinking to solve complex business problems.

I am deeply passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, technology, entrepreneurship, and building products that can make a real difference in people's lives. Every day, I wake up excited about the possibilities that technology unlocks.

Why Computer Science?

Ever since I began exploring computer science, I’ve been fascinated by how structured logic can be used to solve deeply complex problems. My interest in AI and machine learning strengthened this curiosity, showing me how technology can transform industries and reshape decision-making. Over time, I realized that creating impact requires more than technical excellence—it also demands strategic thinking, business understanding, and the ability to navigate people and markets. That realization led me to pursue management, where I aim to bridge technology and business to drive meaningful change.

My Aspiration

I aspire to become an AI engineer and tech entrepreneur who builds products that are not just technically impressive but genuinely useful. I dream of creating solutions that bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world impact. The journey of a thousand miles starts with a single step — and this is mine.
